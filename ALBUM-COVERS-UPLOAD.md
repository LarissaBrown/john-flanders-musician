## How to Upload

### Step 1: Download Images from Old Site
1. Go to [http://www.johnflanders.com/recordings.html](http://www.johnflanders.com/recordings.html)
2. Right-click on each album cover image
3. Save each image to your computer with the filenames listed above

### Step 2: Upload to Admin Dashboard
1. Log in to your admin dashboard at `/admin/login`
2. Navigate to **Images** section
3. Upload each album cover with the exact filename specified above
4. The images will automatically appear in the Discography section

## Technical Details

The Discography component expects:
- **File format:** JPG (preferred) or PNG
- **Aspect ratio:** Square (1:1) works best
- **Recommended size:** At least 500x500px for good quality
- **Location:** Images will be stored in `/public/images/`
