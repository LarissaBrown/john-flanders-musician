## Color Theory Applied

### 1. 60-30-10 Rule
- **60%**: Warm neutrals (backgrounds)
- **30%**: Soft terracottas (brand elements)
- **10%**: Sunset coral (CTAs, highlights)

### 2. Analogous Harmony
- Warm browns ‚Üí Terracottas ‚Üí Coral
- Creates visual unity
- Professional yet warm

### 3. WCAG AA Compliant
- All text: 4.5:1+ contrast ratio
- Accessible to all users
- Readable in any lighting

### 4. Psychological Impact
- **Terracotta**: Creativity, authenticity
- **Warm Neutrals**: Sophistication, trust
- **Coral Accents**: Action, warmth, energy
