## üéØ New Design System

### Color Palette (Dark + Gold)

```css
/* DARK SOPHISTICATION */
Background:       #1C1612  (Deep Charcoal Brown)
Surface:          #2D241E  (Rich Dark Brown - cards)
Surface Elevated: #3A2F28  (Elevated elements)

/* GOLDEN PREMIUM */
Gold Primary:     #F6B800  (Vibrant Gold - CTAs, branding)
Gold Dark:        #D9A300  (Hover states)
Gold Light:       #FFCA28  (Highlights)
Gold Muted:       #C9A55C  (Subtle accents)

/* TEXT */
Primary Text:     #F5F0E8  (Warm Off-White)
Secondary Text:   #B8AFA3  (Muted Tan)
Tertiary Text:    #8A8078  (Subtle Brown)
```

### Typography Hierarchy

```
Brand (JOFLA):    96-128px - Black weight, tight tracking
Headings:         32-48px  - Bold, uppercase, gold
Subheadings:      24-32px  - Bold, uppercase
Body Large:       20-24px  - Light/Regular
Body:             16-18px  - Regular
Small:            12-14px  - Light, wide tracking
```

### Key Design Elements

1. **JOFLA Brand Logo**
   - Geometric circles (3) like MUBI
   - Gold color
   - Huge typography
   - Wide letter-spacing

2. **Dark Background**
   - Deep charcoal brown (#1C1612)
   - Sedona image at 20% opacity (subtle)
   - Creates premium, sophisticated feel

3. **Gold Accent Color**
   - Vibrant #F6B800
   - Used for: Logo, CTAs, headings, highlights
   - High contrast against dark background

4. **MUBI-Style CTA**
   - "LET'S GO" button
   - Bold, uppercase
   - Gold background
   - Rounded rectangle (not fully rounded)

5. **Minimalist Layout**
   - Centered content
   - Generous whitespace
   - Clean, uncluttered
   - Bold typography hierarchy
